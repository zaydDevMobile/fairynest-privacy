🆕 Free App — Banner Ad Supported
🛡️ Summary: FairyNest is a free app supported by a single banner advertisement. It does not collect any personal data from children. All data stays on the device. No accounts, no tracking, no third-party data sharing.

1. Who We Are

FairyNest is a free children's bedtime story app. We are fully committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families.

2. Advertising

FairyNest is a free application supported by a single banner advertisement displayed at the bottom of the home screen. We use Google AdMob to display this ad. The ad is:

  • ✅ Displayed only on the home screen — never during story reading
  • ✅ Family-safe (rated G, child-directed content only)
  • ✅ Non-personalized (no behavioral targeting)
  • ✅ COPPA compliant — no personal data used for ad targeting
  • ❌ No interstitial or full-screen ads
  • ❌ No video ads
  • ❌ No rewarded ads

AdMob may collect certain device information to serve ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Information We Collect

FairyNest itself collects no personal information from users:

  • ❌ No names, emails or phone numbers
  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No photos or camera access
  • ❌ No usage analytics or tracking by us
  • ❌ No behavioral profiling by us

4. Data Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on your device and never transmitted to any server:

  • ✅ Child profile names and avatars (chosen by parent)
  • ✅ Favorite stories
  • ✅ Game scores and reading progress
  • ✅ Language preference

This data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in device settings.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR)

FairyNest is designed for children under 13. We fully comply with COPPA and GDPR-K:

  • We do not collect personal information from children
  • We do not require account registration
  • Ads are child-directed and non-personalized (COPPA compliant)
  • We do not share any user data with third parties
  • We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDK

6. Local Notifications

FairyNest may send local notifications (e.g., bedtime story reminders). These are generated entirely on your device with no external server involved. You can disable them anytime in device settings.

7. Third-Party Services

FairyNest uses Google AdMob solely to display a single banner ad. No other third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services are used. The app functions fully offline after installation (excluding ad loading).

8. Data Security

All user data is stored locally on your device. We have no access to your data. Standard device security protections apply.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

We respond within 48 hours.
