💎 Paid App — No Ads, Ever
🛡️ Summary: FairyNest is a paid app with no advertisements. It does not collect any personal data from children. All data stays on the device. No accounts, no tracking, no third-party sharing.

1. Who We Are

FairyNest is a premium children's bedtime story app. We are fully committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families.

2. Paid App — No Advertising

FairyNest is a paid application. We do not display any advertisements — no banners, no interstitials, no rewarded ads. Your child will never be exposed to advertising content of any kind. We do not use any advertising SDK (including Google AdMob).

3. Information We Collect

FairyNest is designed with privacy by default. We collect no personal information whatsoever:

  • ❌ No names, emails or phone numbers
  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No photos or camera access
  • ❌ No device identifiers
  • ❌ No usage analytics or tracking
  • ❌ No behavioral profiling

4. Data Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on your device and never transmitted to any server:

  • ✅ Child profile names and avatars (chosen by parent)
  • ✅ Favorite stories
  • ✅ Game scores and reading progress
  • ✅ Coloring drawings
  • ✅ Language preference

This data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in device settings.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR)

FairyNest is designed for children under 13. We fully comply with COPPA and GDPR-K:

  • We do not collect personal information from children
  • We do not require account registration
  • We display no advertising of any kind
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDK

6. Local Notifications

FairyNest may send local notifications (e.g., bedtime story reminders). These are generated entirely on your device with no external server involved. You can disable them anytime in device settings.

7. Third-Party Services

FairyNest does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app functions fully offline after installation.

8. Purchases

FairyNest is purchased through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google or Apple. We do not receive, store, or process any payment information.

9. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally and no data is transmitted over the internet, standard device security protections apply. We have no access to your data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact us:
